NIA'S ART WORK of 2024

A collection of my 2024 artwork, featuring watercolor pencil interpretations from museums, quick 1-3 hour acrylic paintings of friends, and spontaneous sketches & paintings capturing fleeting moments

I'm a small independent artist from a small Lithuanian town, immersed in the creative energy and diverse experiences of London. No matter how busy life gets with work and studies, I make time to create—every year, even just a little.

As a creative human being, this year I've explored many passions—art, bachata and salsa dancing, traveling, acro yoga, programming, and even studying German and Spanish. I love to learn, grow, and experience everything life has to offer. This is a glimpse of my artwork from the past year—I hope you enjoy it!
